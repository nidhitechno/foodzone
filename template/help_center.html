help

Welcome to the FoodZone Help Center! We're here to assist you with orders, deliveries, payments, and anything else you need.

1.Ordering & Delivery

• How do I place an order?

Just browse your favorite restaurant, add items to your cart, and click “Checkout.” Choose your address and payment method to complete the order.

• How long will my delivery take?

times depend on the restaurant and your location. Estimated times are shown at checkout.

• Can I schedule an order in advance?

! Select “Schedule Later” at checkout to choose a future time.

• My food hasn’t arrived. What do I do?

Go to “My Orders” and check the status. If it’s delayed, you’ll see real-time updates. If it’s very late, contact our support team.

2.Payments & Refunds

• What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit/debit cards, paypalapp, in selected areas.

• I was charged but didn’t get my order.

We’re sorry! Please contact support with your order number. We’ll investigate and refund if applicable.

• How do I apply a promo code?

Enter your promo code on the checkout page before confirming payment.

3.Restaurant & Menu

• A restaurant is missing. How do I suggest one?

Tap “Suggest a Restaurant” under the Help menu or contact us directly.

• I have a food allergy. Can I make special requests?

Yes, add special instructions on the item or contact the restaurant directly before ordering.

4. Login & Register

•Forgot your password?

Click on "Forgot Password?" on the login page. We'll email you a link to reset it.

•Wrong email or password?

Double-check your credentials. Passwords are case-sensitive.

•Account locked?

For security reasons, your account might be temporarily locked after too many failed attempts. Try again after a few minutes or reset your password.

5.Create an Account

•New to FoodZone?

Click on "Register" and fill in your name, email, and password to create a new account. It only takes a minute!

•Email already in use?

Try logging in or use the forgot password link if you already have an account.

•Not receiving the confirmation email?

Check your spam or junk folder. Still nothing? Try resending the email from the registration page.
